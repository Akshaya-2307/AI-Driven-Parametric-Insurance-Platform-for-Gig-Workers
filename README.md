# AI-Driven Parametric Insurance Platform for Gig Workers

## Overview
This project is developed as part of Guidewire DEVTrails 2026. It focuses on building an AI-driven parametric insurance platform designed specifically for gig delivery workers in India.

Gig workers often face income loss due to external disruptions such as heavy rain, pollution, and sudden curfews. Since their earnings are directly dependent on daily work, these disruptions can significantly impact their financial stability. Currently, there is no reliable and accessible system to protect their income during such events.

This platform aims to provide automated income protection using AI, real-time data, and parametric insurance principles.

---

## Problem Statement
Platform-based delivery partners (food, grocery, e-commerce) experience:

- Loss of income due to environmental and social disruptions  
- Reduced working hours during extreme conditions  
- No structured financial protection system  
- High dependency on daily earnings  

---

## Proposed Solution
We propose an AI-driven parametric insurance system that:

- Assesses risk dynamically using real-time and historical data  
- Calculates affordable premiums on a weekly basis  
- Automatically triggers claims when predefined conditions are met  
- Processes payouts instantly without manual intervention  

---

## Key Features

### AI-Based Risk Assessment
- Uses data such as weather conditions, pollution levels, and location  
- Predicts likelihood of disruptions  
- Enables dynamic premium calculation  

### Weekly Premium Model
- Aligns with the income cycle of gig workers  
- Provides flexible and affordable coverage  

### Parametric Claim Triggers
- Detects real-world disruption events  
- Initiates claims automatically without user input  

### Fraud Detection System
- Identifies abnormal or suspicious claim patterns  
- Validates location and activity data  
- Prevents duplicate or false claims  

---

## Disruptions Covered

- Heavy Rain and Flooding  
- Extreme Heat Conditions  
- Air Pollution Spikes  
- Local Curfews and Restrictions  
- Strikes and Area Shutdowns  

---

## Workflow

1. User registers as a delivery partner  
2. System collects location and operational data  
3. AI model evaluates risk and calculates weekly premium  
4. System continuously monitors external conditions  
5. If disruption criteria are met, claim is triggered automatically  
6. Payout is processed instantly through integrated payment systems
